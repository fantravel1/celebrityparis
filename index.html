Fashion Capital of the World

Paris Fashion Week, Haute Couture, and the front rows that define style

For over a century, Paris has set the global standard for fashion. From the ateliers of Chanel and Dior to the modern spectacles of Balenciaga and Louis Vuitton, the city's runways have drawn the world's most famous faces. Every season, celebrities descend upon the Tuileries, the Palais de Tokyo, and countless other venues to witness the art of couture firsthand.

Paris on Screen

Where cinema history was made

Paris is not merely a backdrop for cinema — it is a character unto itself. From the Lumière brothers' first projections to today's Cannes-adjacent premieres, the city has shaped the art of filmmaking while attracting the greatest actors, directors, and storytellers from around the world.

🎥

Film Premieres

The grand cinemas of the Champs-Élysées and Le Grand Rex have hosted legendary premieres, drawing A-list stars to Paris for decades.

🎬

Paris as a Set

From Midnight in Paris to Amélie, discover the iconic filming locations where Hollywood and French cinema have immortalized the city.

🎧

French New Wave

Godard, Truffaut, Varda — the movement that revolutionized cinema was born on the streets of Paris. Trace the cafes and cinemas where it all began.
